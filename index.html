PRODUCTS

Carton products refer to a wide range of items that are made from cardboard or paperboard material, which are often used for packaging or storage purposes. Carton products come in various shapes and sizes, from simple boxes and containers to more complex designs such as carton tubes, trays, and sleeves. Carton products are versatile, lightweight, and eco-friendly, making them a popular choice for businesses and consumers alike. They can be customized with branding and labeling, making them an effective marketing tool for companies. Additionally, carton products can be easily recycled or biodegraded, reducing their impact on the environment. Examples of carton products include cereal boxes, milk cartons, pizza boxes, shoeboxes, and shipping containers. Overall, carton products offer a cost-effective and sustainable solution for a wide range of packaging and storage needs.

These are specialty tapes, very tough and durable used for sealing, heating & ventilation ducts. They are available in different packing.

Strecch Film

Stretch film, also known as stretch wrap, is a highly stretchable plastic film commonly used in packaging and shipping industries to secure and protect items during transit. It is typically made of linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) or polyethylene (PE) resins, which make it strong and durable while also allowing it to stretch up to several hundred percent of its original size.

Papper Core

Paper cores are made from multiple layers of cardboard that are glued together to form a sturdy tube. The thickness and diameter of the tube can vary depending on the specific application, and paper cores can be customized with various features such as end plugs, labels, and coatings to meet the needs of the product being stored or transported.

Aluminum Tape

It is commonly used in construction, automotive, and electrical applications due to its excellent thermal conductivity, moisture resistance, and heat and light reflectivity. Aluminum tape is typically used for sealing and joining aluminum-backed insulation products, such as ductwork, pipes, and HVAC systems, to prevent air and moisture leakage.

Masking Tape

Masking tape is a type of pressure-sensitive adhesive tape that is commonly used in painting, construction, and crafting applications. It is made from a thin and easy-to-tear paper or plastic backing material that is coated with a low-tack adhesive on one side.

Bopp Tape

BOPP tape, also known as biaxially-oriented polypropylene tape, is a type of packaging tape that is widely used for sealing boxes, packages, and cartons. It is made from a thin film of polypropylene that has been stretched in both the lengthwise and crosswise directions, giving it excellent tensile strength and durability.
